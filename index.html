25hrclock

For the circardian challenged, a clock that resets every 25 hours
download .ZIPdownload .TGZ

Why

Most people have 24 hours circardian cycle. But some rare folks end up having non 24 hr circardian cycle. I started to do a 25 hour cycle just to track what it means. For that I needed a clock that actually follows a 25 hr cycle instead of the usual 24 hours.

Here is the clock. It stores the start time in browser local storage so you can reopen the page and see the correct times.
